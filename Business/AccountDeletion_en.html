Account Deletion Policy Agreement

 

Thank you for using the services of this APP. To protect your rights and ensure you clearly understand matters related to account deletion, please carefully read this policy agreement. When you choose to delete your account, it is deemed that you have read, understood, and agreed to all the terms of this agreement.

 

1. Definition of Account Deletion

Account deletion refers to the operation where you actively choose to permanently delete the current account and all associated data. Once the deletion is completed, you will no longer be able to use this account to log in to this APP or access any related services.

 

2. Consequences of Account Deletion

Termination of Account and Services

- Upon confirming account deletion, your data will be stored for 7 days. If you don't log in during this period, it will be permanently erased from our database thereafter.

- Subscribed services will be terminated immediately. Since this APP currently has no paid content, there will be no additional fees or refund situations.

 

Data Deletion

- All data related to your account, including but not limited to personal information (such as nickname, avatar, etc.), chat history, and uploaded content, will be permanently erased.

- Please back up the data you consider important on your own before deleting the account, as once the data is deleted, it cannot be restored.

 

3. Explanation of Data Deletion

Regular Data Deletion

After you submit the deletion application, the vast majority of your data will be completely deleted from our servers within 24 hours to ensure maximum protection of your privacy.

 

Special Data Retention

Due to compliance requirements or legal obligations, certain data (such as chat records, system logs, etc.) may need to be retained for a certain period in accordance with relevant laws and regulations. The retained data will be kept strictly confidential and used only within the scope permitted by law.

 

4. Irreversible Operation

Account deletion is an irreversible operation. Once the deletion is completed, your account and all related data cannot be restored. Please consider carefully before making a decision. If you only wish to temporarily stop using the account, it is recommended that you choose the "Deactivate Account" function instead of direct deletion.

 

5. Ways to Delete the Account

Self - Service Deletion

You can go to the Me - Security Settings page and follow the system prompts to perform self - service account deletion. Before submitting the deletion application, the system will remind you of the consequences of deletion again and require you to confirm the operation.

 

Contact Customer Service

If you encounter any problems during the deletion process or need further assistance, you can contact our customer service team at any time. The customer service staff will assist you in completing the deletion process.

 

6. Policy Changes

This APP reserves the right to modify this policy at any time. If there are significant changes to this policy, we will notify you via email or in - app notifications. After the policy changes come into effect, if you continue to use the services of this APP, it is deemed that you have accepted the new policy terms.
