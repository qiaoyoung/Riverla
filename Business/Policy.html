Account Deactivation Policy Agreement

 

     Thank you for using the services of our APP. To protect your rights and ensure you are aware of the consequences of deactivating your account, please carefully read the following policy agreement. When you choose to deactivate your account, it indicates that you have read, understood, and agreed to all terms of this agreement.

1. Definition of Account Deactivation

Account deactivation refers to your active choice to temporarily stop using your account and related services of our App. After deactivation, your account will enter a "frozen" state, and you will be unable to log in or use any functions. However, your account data will be temporarily retained in the system.

2. Consequences of Account Deactivation

Service Termination

- After deactivation, you will not be able to log in or use any functions of this app.

- Subscribed services will be automatically terminated, and no additional fees will be incurred.

Data Retention

- Your account data (including personal profiles, chat historical records, etc.) will be temporarily retained during the deactivation period but cannot be accessed or restored.

- If you want to permanently delete your data, please select the "Delete Account" operation.

Recovery Period

- You can restore your account within 30 days after deactivation by re - logging in or contacting customer service.

- After 30 days, your account and data will be permanently deleted and cannot be restored.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Data Retention

- Due to compliance requirements or legal obligations, certain data (such as chat records, logs, etc.) may need to be retained for a certain period.

- The retained data will be strictly in accordance with relevant laws and regulations and will only be used for legal or regulatory purposes when necessary.

Data Deletion

- If you choose to permanently delete your account, all data related to the account will be cleared from our servers within 24 hours.

- The deletion operation is irreversible. Please choose carefully.

4. Methods of Account Deactivation

Self - Service Deactivation

- You can deactivate your account through the "Security Settings" page of APP.

- Before deactivation, the system will prompt you to confirm the operation and read this policy agreement.

Contacting Customer Service

- If you need assistance, please contact our customer service team

5. User Responsibilities

Account Security

- After deactivating your account, you need to properly safeguard your account information to prevent unauthorized restoration or use of your account by others.

Data Backup

- It is recommended that you back up important data (such as chat records, uploaded content, etc.) on your own before deactivating your account. Our app is not responsible for data loss caused by account deactivation.

6. Policy Changes

Our App reserves the right to modify this policy at any time. In case of major changes, we will notify you via email or in - app notifications. Continuing to use our services means that you agree to accept the updated policy.
